why ill never be a normie

by seraph @ 20:20PM | 27.04.2026

thumbnail titled "why ill never be a normie"

being "weird" is popular, being *weird* is not popular

i think people often forget that all the good in our society as we see it today was made possible because of those weirdos, the rejects, the nerds, the deviants - all the people that society calls "creepy", "deranged", and "strange" are some of the most important people of all

and thats what it really comes down to - society decides who gets to succeed, and who gets to fail because no matter how smart, creative, and innovative u are, if u arent a normie u will be trampled on

the normies are ganging up against me, and many other intelligent people - they mark us excommunicado from public life because we do not fit their brainwashed concept of what is and isn't acceptable but here is the reality: normies hate us because we are different, because we scare them, because they cant control us

and if they get their way, and they push us all into a police state of normality - it will destroy any chance we have at freedom and rule of the people, it will be despotism

a video from sid meier's alpha centauri explaining how we need to protect our access to freedom of information

so what can we even do about this ?

be urself - it sounds cheesy as FUCK but its true !! literally nobody can climb into ur head and stop u from being urself and the best part ? being urself attracts OTHER people who are weird so u can make weird friends

then what ? build communities that protect eachother, that comfort eachother, that share information with eachother

many assume that in order to be an activist you must march down streets with banners, shout slogans, and make a scene - and whilst that too is great, you can desist in ways that require far less effort but are effective in their own rights

by existing, by refusing to give up and perish, your very presence is mightier than any sword and stories of your courage to be yourself even when faced with the ravenous rage of men in suits will be spoken of for generations

a post on bluesky by seraph (seraphmoe)

so, to the many who told me to "stop being weird", blow me <3

and for the weirdos who are reading this ? i love you, never stop being weird, im so proud of you, you deserve love and care and comfort, and someday this witchhunt will be over, you will be safe, and you will be 100% unapolegetically yourself, you will be free
